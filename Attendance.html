Case Study

Employee Management System

A Mobile App for Smarter Workforce Management

Role: End-to-End Design (UI/UX, Branding, Prototyping)

Timeline: Jun 2025 â€“ Jul 2025

Tools: Figma, FigJam, Illustrator

Overview

The EMS Mobile App streamlines employee services like Leave, Attendance, Payslip, Complaints, Chat, and Birthday Reminders. It is designed to improve workplace efficiency and communication in one integrated platform.

The Challenge

Traditional HR and employee service portals are often outdated, hard to navigate, and lack personalization. Employees face delays in leave approvals, payroll queries, and complaint resolutions. The challenge was to build a mobile-first, modern EMS that simplifies daily tasks for employees and HR teams.

    Provide quick access to Leave, Attendance, and Payslip

    Enable direct Complaint and Chat system for better communication

    Offer birthday reminders and employee engagement tools

My Role

    UI/UX Designer: Wireframes, prototypes, user flows

    Brand Styling: Defined type, color scheme, iconography

    User Testing: Conducted employee trials for usability

Process

Research

Studied common HRMS apps, noting gaps in usability and features employees wanted most, like one-click payslip downloads and real-time attendance tracking.

Wireframes & IA

Planned flows for Leave Requests, Attendance Logs, Payslip Access, Complaints, and Birthday Reminders. Ensured minimum taps to complete each action.

User Flow

The app flow separated Employee and HR/Admin journeys, ensuring each role had tailored dashboards, notifications, and reporting tools.

Visual Design

Typography: Clean sans-serif for clarity

Color Palette: Blue palette for trust

Visuals: Icons, cards, and interactive charts

UI Highlights

Outcome

The EMS app simplified employee-HR interactions with modern UI and real-time features. Early testing showed 70% faster leave approvals and improved employee satisfaction. Future scope includes integrating AI for attendance predictions and payroll insights.
