Case Study

The Verse Perch

A Digital Haven for Poets & Poetry Lovers

Role: End-to-End Design (UI/UX, Branding, Prototyping)

Timeline: Apr 2025 – May 2025

Tools: Figma, FigJam, Illustrator

Overview

The Verse Perch is a sanctuary for poetic souls — A platform where voices are heard, stories are shared, and art finds its rhythm in design.

The Challenge

Most Poetry platforms today are either outdated, text-heavy, or lack the emotional depth that poetry deserves. While some allow users to publish poems, very few offer interactive features like live events, personalized poet profiles, or true audience engagement. My challenge was to build a soulful yet practical platform that connects creators and listeners through a focused, intuitive experience.

    Craft a calm and artistic aesthetic true to poetry

    Enable smooth discovery of poets, events, and blogs that help users learn poetic techniques

    Design a scalable layout that can later extend to tablet and mobile

Note: While this version focuses on desktop, the layout was designed to adapt easily to mobile and iPad screens in the next iteration.

My Role

    UI/UX Designer: Wireframes, design system, and prototyping

    Brand Styling: Defined type, color palette, and visual tone

    User Testing: Gathered iterative feedback for refinements

Process

Research & Inspiration

I explored editorial poetry websites and minimalist layouts that enhance literary appeal. These informed layout spacing, type hierarchy, and mood.

Wireframes & IA

I structured the experience around user roles – Poets and Listeners – with flows focused on discovery and participation. The sitemap included: Home, Poets, Events, Blogs, Subscriptions, Sign Up.

User Flow

The user flow clearly separates journeys for Poets and Listeners—from onboarding to goal completion. It ensures users can navigate smoothly, whether they want to share poetry or attend events.

Visual Design

Typography: Serif + Sans pairing for expressive clarity

Color Palette: Earthy greens and soft beiges

Visuals: Clean cards, poetic illustrations, smooth CTA hierarchy

Design Strategy Q&A

A clear hero section with a poetic tagline and CTA highlights the platform’s core purpose within seconds.

I used distinct sign-up pathways, role-based dashboards, and navigation tailored to each user type.

I designed soft CTAs, non-intrusive subscription prompts, and emphasized the poetic content over commercial elements.

Most platforms are either blog-heavy or visually outdated. I solved this with clean layout, interaction flow, and stronger personalization.

I paired elegant serif typography, warm tones, and quotes throughout to allow users to feel immersed in a poetic narrative.

UI Highlights

Outcome

The Verse Perch offers a storytelling-rich user experience that blends performance, learning, and creative exploration in poetry. Its simple navigation and calm aesthetic make it accessible and inspiring for all users.

With positive feedback from poetry enthusiasts, the next phase involves integrating AI-based poetry recommendations and live event hosting.
