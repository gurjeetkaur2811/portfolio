Hey there, I'm Gurjeet Kaur

A passionate UI/UX Designer with a curious mind and a love for turning ideas into intuitive experiences.

My journey began during my B.Tech in Computer Science, where I started exploring creativity through freelance logo design projects alongside my studies. It was in my final semester that everything changed, I joined Covalience India as an intern, diving deep into HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web technologies.

While I enjoyed coding, I found myself more drawn toward the visual side of development like the layout, the colors, the interaction. That’s when I discovered the world of UI/UX design, and I knew I had found my space. I shifted my internship focus toward UI/UX and never looked back.

What started as curiosity turned into a full-time role. I officially became a UI/UX Designer at Covalience, and now, 2 years later, I still feel the same excitement I felt on day one. Every screen I design is a blend of problem-solving, empathy, and creativity and that’s why I can proudly say:
I’m a passionate UI/UX Designer.
